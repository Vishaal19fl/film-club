our services

direction

The portrayal of the director can be phrased by the formula: Cinematography + Setting + Blocking + Acting + Editing = Directing. The Way In Which It Is Done Drastically Affects The Outcome And Success Of The Film. Apply to join our team as a director.

acting

Acting is the art of storytelling. You take a script and breathe life into a character, portraying it on a stage or screen. It Provokes Emotion, Thought, Discussion, Awareness, Or Even Imagination. Apply to join us a an actor.

screen writing

The Screenplay Is The Guide For The Producers, Director, Actors, Crew Of Exactly What Will Appear On Screen. It Is The Common Ground That Everybody On The Film Will Work With From Start To Finish Of Production. Apply to join us as a screenwriter.
